Looking to lend a helping hand?

Check out a few of our initiatives by the community!

Programming lessons for under-privileged children

Led by "Coding from the Heart"

As the world of Information Technology advances, it is clear that our future will be built upon technology. As such, to give the under-privileged children a headstart in this future, Coding From The Heart hopes to gather proficient programmers to educate these children on the power of programming so that it may some day change their lives for the better.

Free physiotherapy sessions for the elderly

Led by "Physio For Good"

It is commonly known that the elderly tend to suffer from pains in their bodies be it from recent falls or a result of past injuries in their youth coming back to haunt them. However, the high cost of physiotherapy sessions in our healthcare centres discourage the elderly from seeking proper treatment. As such, Physio For Good hopes to gather certified physiotherapists to offer free sessions to keep our golden generation in the pink of health!

Building computers for the under-privileged

Led by "Scrap Builders"

As the usage of computers becomes more prevalent in society, it is hard to ignore the fact that it is arguably a basic necessity in today's world. However, there are still many under-privileged Singaporeans without computers. As such, Scrap Builders hopes to gather Computer-building enthusiasts to source for outdated or discarded computer parts which are still reusable to build computers for the under-privileged.

WHAT WE DO

OneSG is an innovative, one-stop platform that aims to make Singapore a more inclusive society. It seeks to seamlessly connect people in need of help or resources to those who can offer them and provide users with an excellent user experience. Some of its features include allowing volunteers to pitch initiatives, receive notifications of the latest suitable volunteering opportunities and get directions to the nearest donation point.
